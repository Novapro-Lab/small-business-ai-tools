# Sales Call Script (Discovery)

A discovery call outline focused on clarity and fit — not pressure. Adjust timing for 20–30 minute calls.

---

## Before the call

- [ ] Review intake form or prior emails  
- [ ] Confirm attendee names and roles  
- [ ] Prepare 2–3 relevant questions based on their industry  
- [ ] Have a simple note template ready  

---

## Opening (2–3 minutes)

> Hi [Name], thanks for making time.  
> Goal for today: understand your situation, see if we're a good fit, and leave you with clear next steps — even if that's "not now."  
> Does that work? Anything you want to make sure we cover?

---

## Context (5–7 minutes)

1. **Situation** — "Walk me through how you handle [process] today."  
2. **Pain** — "What's most frustrating or costly about that?"  
3. **Impact** — "If this stayed the same for 12 months, what would that cost you?"  
4. **Prior attempts** — "What have you already tried?"

*Listen more than you talk. Take notes in their words.*

---

## Goals and constraints (5–7 minutes)

5. **Success** — "What would a good outcome look like in 90 days?"  
6. **Timeline** — "Is there a date or event driving urgency?"  
7. **Budget** — "Do you have a rough range allocated, or is that still open?"  
8. **Decision process** — "Who else weighs in before moving forward?"

---

## Fit and approach (5 minutes)

Summarize back:

> Here's what I heard: [2–3 bullets]. Did I get that right?

If fit is reasonable:

> Based on that, a sensible next step could be [proposal / technical review / pilot scope].  
> I won't promise anything we haven't scoped — I'll follow up in writing with options.

If not a fit:

> Honestly, we may not be the best match because [reason]. I'd suggest [alternative]. Happy to stay in touch if things change.

---

## Closing (2 minutes)

- Confirm next step, owner, and date  
- Thank them for their time  
- Send summary email within 24 hours (see [lead-follow-up-template.md](./lead-follow-up-template.md))

---

## After the call

| Item | Owner | Due |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Summary email | | +24h |
| CRM update | | Same day |
| Internal debrief (if needed) | | +48h |

---

## What to avoid

- Talking over the prospect  
- Demoing before understanding the problem  
- Guaranteed ROI without data  
- Sharing other clients' confidential details
