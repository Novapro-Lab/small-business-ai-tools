# Lead Follow-Up Template

Use this template for consistent, respectful follow-up after inbound interest or a prior conversation.

---

## Email / message structure

**Subject:** Following up — [topic or company name]

Hi [First name],

Thank you for [how they reached out: form, referral, event, call].

I'm following up on [specific problem or request in one sentence].

**What I understood:**
- Situation: [their context]
- Goal: [what they want to achieve]
- Timing: [their stated timeline, if any]

**Suggested next step:**
[One clear option — e.g. 20-minute call, async questionnaire, demo]

[Optional: one sentence of relevant value — case type, not a hard sell]

If the timing isn't right, just reply and I'll note that. No need to stay on the list.

Best regards,  
[Your name]  
[Role] · [Company]  
[Contact method]

---

## Internal checklist (before sending)

- [ ] Personalized at least two fields (name, company, problem)
- [ ] One clear call-to-action — not three competing asks
- [ ] No exaggerated claims or guaranteed outcomes
- [ ] Sent within agreed SLA (e.g. 1 business day for inbound)
- [ ] Logged in CRM or tracker with date and next step

---

## Follow-up cadence (suggested)

| Touch | Timing | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Day 0 | Acknowledge and propose next step |
| 2 | Day 3–5 | Short reminder with one new detail |
| 3 | Day 10–14 | Post-call summary or value-add resource |
| 4 | Day 21+ | Close-the-loop message if no response |

Adjust cadence to your industry norms and consent requirements.
